My dream home

My dream home is a classic New York City brownstone, nestled on a quiet, tree-lined street with ivy creeping up its historic façade. There's something timeless and grounding about the architecture—each creaky floorboard and tall window tells a story. But the heart of this home is the library: a cozy, light-filled room with floor-to-ceiling bookshelves, a sliding ladder, and a worn leather armchair by the window. In a city that never stops moving, this space offers stillness—a personal sanctuary where stories from every corner of the world live within arms reach. It's the perfect blend of urban energy and quiet retreat.
